View Main

Displaying this research is a necessary expression of awareness on how differing populations receive beneficial assistance over others. There are several ways to present this information, what is called opportunity mapping and this research will utilize five individualized measures, combine these data points into a composite index and display juxtaposed data through a map of King County. This research will attempt to further enlighten its readers through visualization of education indicators based on census data from 2010 through 2012.
